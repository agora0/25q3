24. Laughter Cream (笑聲面霜)

Topic: 24. Laughter Cream

Yoga Class Notes: One of 40 Foundation Exercises

In the Laughter Yoga class, there's a part called "Laughter Cream." I like this exercise because it's like "beautifying" myself! We pretend to squeeze laughter cream from a tube or scoop it out of a jar. First, I rub it on my hands, and then I naturally pretend to apply it.

The students in front of the camera, including me, are never serious when applying it. We all use exaggerated expressions and movements. Some people wrinkle their faces as if the cream is too cold. Others shake their whole bodies as if the cream is too sticky. Some even make "glug glug" sounds, as if they're eating the cream! Or as if the cream is eating us! Haha!

It feels very simple, like playing with the air as a child. You don't have to think too much, just do it. Even though there's nothing in our hands, just pretending is funny, and then we all laugh together.

What is this? It's the most magical "skincare product." After applying it, it not only protects our skin, but also instantly brightens our mood.


24. 笑聲面霜

瑜珈課筆記：40個基礎練習之一

（大笑瑜珈） 課有個環節叫（歡笑潤膚霜），我喜歡這個動作, 因為可以（扮靚!） 。這是假裝從一條管子裡擠出，或者從罐子裡挖出一點（歡笑霜）。我先在自己手上抹開，然後很自然地假裝塗抹.

鏡頭前的同學, 包括我, 塗抹的時候都不會正經的，我們都會搭配誇張的表情和動作。有的人會擠眉弄眼，好像乳霜太冰；有的人會全身抖動，好像乳霜太黏；還有人會發出（咕嚕咕嚕）的聲音，好像在吃乳霜！或者乳霜會吃掉我們! 哈哈!

這感覺很單純，就像小時候對著空氣玩耍一樣，不用想太多，做就對了。雖然手上什麼都沒有，就是假裝, 就是好笑，然後一起哈哈大笑。

這是什麼? 這是最神奇的（保養品），塗上去之後，不僅可是護膚, 心情馬上就亮了起來。
